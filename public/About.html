About Page

Purpose

EmpowerLink is a job search platform designed to support women of color navigating early career opportunities.

Why It Exists

Women of color often face barriers in career access, visibility, and representation. This platform centers their experience.

What It Does

EmpowerLink aggregates public job listings and provides tools to save and organize opportunities.

Future Vision

Future versions may include curated resources, mentorship links, and community-driven features.

EmpowerLink is dedicated to connecting women of color with equitable career opportunities.
